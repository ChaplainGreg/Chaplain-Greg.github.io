About Bible Study Chaplaincy

About Me

I serve as a chaplain with a heart for expository Bible teaching and peer support for law enforcement and other first responders. With seminary training and real-world ministry experience, my calling is to walk beside those who serve and protect, offering Scripture-based guidance, hope, and encouragement.

Expository Bible Study

This section will regularly feature in-depth, verse-by-verse explorations of Scripture. The goal is to equip believers—especially those in high-stress roles—with solid, Spirit-filled understanding of God’s Word.

Coming soon: studies in Psalms, Romans, and John.

Chaplaincy & Peer Support

I provide confidential, faith-based peer support to law enforcement personnel, fire/rescue teams, EMS workers, and their families. My role includes spiritual care, grief support, stress management, and crisis ministry grounded in biblical principles and professional ethics.
